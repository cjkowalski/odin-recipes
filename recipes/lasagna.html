Lasagna Recipe


Description:

Prepare to embark on a culinary journey with a classic lasagna recipe that promises to delight your senses. Begin by crafting a robust meat sauce, where tender ground beef and sweet Italian sausage mingle with onions, garlic, and tomatoes, infused with a harmonious blend of Italian herbs. Simmered to perfection, this sauce forms the flavorful foundation of your lasagna, setting the stage for a symphony of tastes to unfold.

Next, elevate your creation with a velvety béchamel sauce, crafted from buttery roux and creamy milk, adding a layer of richness between tender lasagna noodles. Assemble your masterpiece by layering the noodles with the meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous sprinkling of Parmesan and mozzarella cheese. Baked to golden perfection, the lasagna emerges from the oven bubbling and fragrant, ready to be served as a timeless classic that brings comfort and joy to any table.


Ingredients

For the meat sauce:
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 pound (225g) sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1/2 pound (225g) sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces/170g) tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the béchamel sauce:
  • 4 tablespoons (56g) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (960ml) whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Additional ingredients:
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
